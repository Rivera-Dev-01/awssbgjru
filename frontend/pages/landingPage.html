Who Are We

AWS Learning Club - JRU Chapter is a newly established AWS Learning Chapter focused on bringing cloud knowledge, innovation, and hands-on learning to students, based at Jose Rizal University

VISION

To build a network of student innovators using AWS to drive technological progress and industry transformation.

MISSION

To empower students through hands-on learning, workshops, and community service that build real AWS and cloud skills.

DEPARTMENTS

Dedicated technical teams that bring our projects, programs, and community initiatives to life.

OFFICES

The leadership teams that guide our chapter's vision, growth, and direction.

What We Do

The AWS Learning Club - JRU Chapter bridges the gap between academic theory and real-world cloud technology. We host peer-to-peer learning sessions, interactive technical workshops, and collaborative community projects centered around cloud computing and innovation.

Fueling Tomorrow's Tech Leaders

We don't just study the cloud—we actively build and innovate within it. The AWS Learning Club - JRU Chapter is a dynamic student community that bridges the gap between academic theory and industry practice. Through hands-on team training, collaborative team projects, and expert-led workshops, we turn theoretical knowledge into real-world skills.
