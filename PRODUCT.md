# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Mapúa University students, cloud enthusiasts, and aspiring tech professionals who want to build real-world skills and connect with a community.

## Product Purpose
The official website for the AWS Student Builder Group Mapúa Chapter. It serves to highlight events, showcase the community, and provide a platform for members to engage with the club's activities and cloud technology.

## Brand Personality
Empowering, Innovative, Student-centric, and Professional.

## Anti-references
Should not look like a generic, dry corporate dashboard. It should feel lively, welcoming, and modern.

## Design Principles
- "Show, don't tell": Highlight actual community achievements and events.
- "Practice what you preach": The website itself should reflect good cloud/web architecture.
- "Community first": Keep the focus on students and learning.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast for readability, clear navigation, and inclusive language suitable for a diverse student body.
